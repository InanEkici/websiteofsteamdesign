Hey there! If you are on this website you have probably been given express permission to be on here. If not, I would that you close this tab NOW!

The purpose of theis site is to be a sort of pseudo library for at the very least some of my extra carricular activities and hobbies. This could range from anything education related, to my hobbies such as gaming and reading. Generally, this should be a website that is focused around my coding challanges, creations and achievements.

..............................

In the first part of my website I will have all of my coding adventures. Hopefully by the time I am done, it wil be a paged filled with many interesting things, such as handy programs. There is a good posibility that I will also have my leaving certificate project up here.

The second part of my websie, will be formated in blog form. This will be focused on my other achievement that are not accosiated with coding. Hopefully it will have news of me making PR in the Irish SSBM tier list. It might also contain my opinions on contemperary events that are ongoing around the world.
